Why Compress Images for Job Applications?

Many online recruitment portals require candidates to upload a recent photograph within a specific file size limit. If your image exceeds the allowed size, the upload may fail or the application form may not be submitted successfully. Compressing your image helps reduce file size while maintaining good visual quality.

Whether you are applying for government jobs, private companies, internships, apprenticeships, banking exams, railway recruitment, police recruitment, teaching positions or campus placements, having an optimized image saves time and avoids unnecessary upload errors.

TinyPixel provides a fast browser-based solution that allows you to compress images without installing any software. The process is simple, secure and works on both desktop and mobile devices.
