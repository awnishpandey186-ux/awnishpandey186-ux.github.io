Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is TinyPixel free to use?

Yes. TinyPixel is completely free to use for image compression and conversion.

Do you upload my images?

No. Your images are processed directly in your browser whenever possible. We do not store your files.

Which image formats are supported?

TinyPixel supports JPG, JPEG, PNG and WebP image formats.

Can I convert PNG to JPG?

Yes. TinyPixel includes a PNG to JPG Converter.

Can I convert JPG to PNG?

Yes. TinyPixel also includes a JPG to PNG Converter.

Can I convert images to WebP?

Yes. You can convert JPG and PNG images into WebP format.

Can I compress images to 50KB or 100KB?

Yes. TinyPixel provides tools to compress images to popular size limits like 50KB and 100KB.

Does TinyPixel work on mobile devices?

Yes. TinyPixel works on Android phones, iPhones, tablets and desktop computers.

Is there any file size limit?

Most images are supported. Extremely large images may take longer depending on your device.

How can I contact TinyPixel?

Please visit the Contact Us page for support or feedback.
