Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find helpful technical documentation and core guidance parameters concerning TinyPixel's browser-side image processing performance architecture below.

Is TinyPixel free to use?

Yes. TinyPixel provides unrestricted access across our total range of image compression utilities and configuration processing options. Users face no paywalls, registration steps, premium service additions, or hidden subscription costs.

Do you upload my private images onto external servers?

No. TinyPixel operates using structural HTML5 canvas structures that handle compression processing routines instantly within your browser runtime context. Your files stay stored strictly on your local computer or phone hardware throughout the operation.

Which image formats are supported natively by TinyPixel?

Our tools are configured to handle the web's standard design formats smoothly, processing all files matching standard JPG, JPEG, transparent PNG backgrounds, and next-generation WebP digital graphic types.

Can I convert PNG assets to standard JPG configurations?

Yes. TinyPixel features a customized PNG to JPG configuration option. The system strips heavy transparency blocks cleanly and reformats your images into optimized baseline JPEG layouts suited for official document uploads.

Can I convert standard JPG images into PNG configurations?

Yes. You can re-encode standard JPG capture layouts straight into structured PNG elements using our integrated converter page without losing critical visual data attributes.

Can I convert older images to modern WebP formats?

Yes. TinyPixel includes dynamic target formatting tools to encode incoming JPG, JPEG, and PNG components into the modernized WebP framework, helping maximize asset performance ratios across live production servers.

Can I compress images down to explicit 50KB or 100KB limits?

Yes. We deploy custom target threshold profiles tailored for popular sizing limits like 20KB, 30KB, 50KB, and 100KB. These targeted pipelines make it easy to modify pictures for specific regional application needs or portals.

Does TinyPixel work uniformly on mobile devices?

Yes. Our client-side framework is fully responsive and runs uniformly across Apple iOS platforms, Android smartphones, tablets, and legacy operating systems without requiring any localized software modifications.

Is there a concrete file upload size limit?

Our client-side processing handles most file configurations seamlessly. However, working with ultra-large source captures (such as 50MB+ RAW digital frames) will rely directly on your local device's memory capacity to finish conversion tasks.

How can I reach out to contact TinyPixel for support?

If you run into issues or have ideas to share, please check out our dedicated Contact Us channel to reach our site managers directly.
