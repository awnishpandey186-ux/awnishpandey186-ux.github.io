WhatsApp Image Compressor

This tool helps you compress images specifically for WhatsApp sharing without losing noticeable quality. Large images often get cropped or compressed automatically in WhatsApp, reducing clarity.

Using TinyPixel, you can optimize images before sending them, ensuring better quality and faster sharing. It supports JPG, PNG, and WebP formats.

Everything runs directly in your browser, so your images remain private and secure. No uploads or storage are involved.

This tool is ideal for sharing photos, documents, and media on WhatsApp groups or chats.
