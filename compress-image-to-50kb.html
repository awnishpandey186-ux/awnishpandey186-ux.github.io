Compress Image to 50KB Online

TinyPixel allows you to compress images to 50KB instantly without losing important quality. This tool is specially designed for users who need small image sizes for online applications, government forms, competitive exams, and official document uploads.

Many websites require images under strict size limits like 20KB, 50KB, or 100KB. Manually reducing image size can be difficult, but this tool automatically compresses your image directly in the browser using advanced canvas processing. No upload is required, ensuring complete privacy.

Supported formats include JPG, PNG, and WebP. You can easily upload your image, adjust quality, and download the compressed version instantly. This tool works on mobile and desktop devices.

Use cases include passport applications, Aadhaar updates, job applications, school forms, and other government or private portals where image size restrictions are strict.
