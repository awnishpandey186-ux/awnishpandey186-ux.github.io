Why Compress Images for Government Job Applications?

Most government recruitment portals require applicants to upload a recent passport-size photograph that meets strict file size and format requirements. Whether you are applying for SSC, UPSC, Railway Recruitment Boards (RRB), IBPS banking exams, State PSC, Police recruitment, Defence services or other public sector jobs, an oversized image can prevent your application from being submitted successfully.

TinyPixel helps you compress your photograph quickly without installing software. The tool reduces file size while maintaining good image quality, making it easier to upload photographs even on slow internet connections.
