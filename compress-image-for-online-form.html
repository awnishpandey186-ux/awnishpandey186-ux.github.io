Why Compress Images for Online Forms?

Online application forms have become the standard method for submitting documents to government departments, educational institutions, banks, recruitment agencies, insurance providers and private organizations. Most portals require applicants to upload a passport-size photograph or supporting documents within a fixed file size limit. If the image exceeds the allowed size, the upload may fail or the application cannot be submitted successfully.

TinyPixel helps reduce image size quickly while keeping photographs clear and readable. Instead of installing editing software, you can compress your image directly in your browser and prepare it for almost any online application.

Whether you are applying for a government service, scholarship, university admission, banking form, employment opportunity or another online registration, compressing your image before uploading saves time and reduces upload errors.

  • File size exceeds the allowed limit.
  • Unsupported image format.

  • Use a recent, clear photograph.
  • Avoid compressing the same image multiple times.

  • Preview the compressed image before downloading.
