About TinyPixel

TinyPixel is a free online image compression and image conversion platform designed to help users reduce image file sizes quickly while maintaining excellent image quality. Our goal is to provide simple, fast, and secure image optimization tools that work directly in your browser without requiring software installation or account registration.

Many websites, government portals, educational institutions, and online application forms require images to meet strict file size limits such as 10KB, 20KB, 30KB, 50KB, 100KB, or 200KB. TinyPixel makes this process easy by providing browser-based tools that help users compress images while keeping them clear and readable.
